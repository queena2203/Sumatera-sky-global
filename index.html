About Us

At Sumatera Sky Global, we specialize in exporting high-quality Cassia cinnamon sourced from the rich forests of Sumatra, Indonesia. Our mission is to bring the natural essence of Indonesian spices to global markets, with a focus on quality, sustainability, and reliability.

We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to ensure consistent standards and fair trade practices. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we are proud to deliver authentic Indonesian cinnamon that meets international expectations.
